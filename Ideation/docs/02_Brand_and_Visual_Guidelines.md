# Brand and Visual Guidelines

## Parent Brand

Always use the full organization name:

**The Wizard Nexus**

Never shorten the company name to “Wizard Nexus” in formal brand presentation.

## Brand Phrase

**Where WiZdom Meets Innovation**

The substitution of **Z** for **s** is intentional and should be subtle. Do not:

- recolor only the word WiZdom unless the composition calls for it;
- add a trademark symbol to the phrase;
- exaggerate the Z into a novelty effect;
- introduce additional spelling changes.

## Product Brand

# ARCANE

Subtitle:

**An AI-Native Operating Environment**

Optional technical expansion:

**Adaptive Runtime for Cognitive AI Native Environments**

The expansion is architecturally accurate but does not need to be pushed as a consumer-facing acronym.

## TWiN Compass™

The ethical and moral foundation is called:

**TWiN Compass™**

Preferred explanation:

> The moral and ethical guidance layer beneath ARCANE.

Avoid replacing the name with generic phrases such as “Moral Engine” when the product name can be used.

## Visual Language

The website reference establishes the primary direction:

- deep black and midnight-blue cosmic backgrounds;
- fine star fields and nebular textures;
- elegant serif display typography;
- restrained warm gold;
- white or ivory body text;
- selective blue, purple, and cyan accents;
- thin borders;
- large negative space;
- calm, premium, astronomical presentation;
- less cyberpunk, less gaming UI, and less crowded enterprise-dashboard styling.

## Layout Rules

- Use a single explicit page grid.
- Header, body, and footer must share the same primary center line.
- Each column must have a consistent width.
- Sections within a column must align to that column.
- A logo area must not push the central title off-center.
- Treat the brand area and central product title as independent grid regions.
- Use guides during final composition rather than relying on apparent visual centering.
- Prefer fewer, stronger messages over dense feature lists.

## Official Logo Handling

When the actual logo cannot be inserted, use a deliberate placeholder:

- hexagonal outline;
- subtle blue-to-gold border;
- no invented wizard, wand, star, or corporate emblem;
- centered label: `OFFICIAL THE WIZARD NEXUS LOGO`;
- visually neutral enough to be replaced later.

The generated concept images are ideation references, not authoritative logo treatments.
