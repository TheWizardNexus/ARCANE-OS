## Philosophy

ARCANE is **not simply another operating system**.

It represents a **new relationship between humans and computing**.

Traditional operating systems organize hardware, files, and
applications.

ARCANE organizes **intent, memory, capabilities, ethics, and human
collaboration**.

The underlying **System Platform** (Windows, Linux, Android, etc.)
becomes an implementation detail rather than the user's experience.
