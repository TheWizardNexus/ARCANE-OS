Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions


Data Storage and Security

All information processed by our AI is stored exclusively on your device. We do not store any data on our servers to ensure the highest level of privacy and security for our users. This means your data will not be synced with other devices. For the best experience, continuity of care, and the AI's train of thought, we strongly recommend using just one device.


Multiple Devices

We understand that some users may have unique situations requiring the use of multiple devices. If you find yourself in such a situation, you can register each device individually. However, please be aware that data will not be shared or synchronized between devices. Each device will function independently to maintain your privacy and security.


Recommendations

For optimal use and to maintain the integrity of your interactions with the AI, it is recommended to:

  • Use a single device whenever possible.
  • Ensure your device is secure and protected to safeguard your data.
  • Ensure your device has a password or uses a secure screenlock.
