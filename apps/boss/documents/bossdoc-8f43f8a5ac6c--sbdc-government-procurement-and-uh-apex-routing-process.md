- If the user is only thinking about starting a business, keep SBDC primary and focus on validation, operations, and readiness.
- If the user is newly selling but has little track record, use Government Jumpstation to educate them by stage.

- Use APEX for registrations, certifications education, market research, bid/no-bid review, solicitation guidance, and government accounting system education.
